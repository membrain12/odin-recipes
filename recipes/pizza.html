Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a classic recipe that everyone should know how to make.

Very simple, but very satisfying

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Sliced Pepperonis

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to temp from box
  2. Add Mozzarella and pepperonis on top
  3. Bake until golden brown crust
  4. Enjoy!
