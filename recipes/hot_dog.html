Hot Dogs

Description

This is a classic recipe that everyone should know how to make.

Very simple, but very satisfying

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Sliced American Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter 2 slices of bread
  2. Place 1 slice butter side down on pan
  3. Put cheese on top
  4. Put remaining piece butter side up on top
  5. Cook until golden brown then flip and repeat
